About the Artist

Todd “Myz” Myz

Welcome to Art by Myz — a space where digital illustration, gaming influences, and bold graphic design collide.

I’m Joshua, a Colorado-based digital artist who loves building stylized characters, merch-ready graphics, and expressive designs inspired by fantasy, sci-fi, and modern pop culture.

My work lives across prints, stickers, apparel, and digital commissions. Whether it’s a single character portrait or a full collection of designs, I strive to make each piece feel intentional, energetic, and uniquely “Myz.”

Thank you for stopping by, supporting independent art, and being part of this creative experiment.
