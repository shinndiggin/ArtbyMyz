About the Artist

Welcome to Art by Myz — a space where digital art, creativity, and imagination take shape. My name is Joshua “Myz” Irvine, a Colorado-based digital artist who specializes in bold illustrations, character concept art, and unique graphic designs inspired by gaming, fantasy, and modern pop culture.

My work blends vibrant color palettes, stylized elements, and a love for expressive character storytelling. Whether I'm creating merch designs, prints, or one-of-a-kind commissions, I pour passion into each piece.

Thank you for supporting my art journey—and welcome to the world of Myz.
